Hi there!

I am a GIScientist & an international development consultant in Tokyo.

In my current role, I have involved in a GIS project for forestory management in Papua New Guinea and a cloud native GIS distribution project.

I completed Master's Program in GIS for Conservation Application at Clark University in 2024, and I wrote a master's thesis about the forest structure and bear sightings.

Before joining the Clark University, I studied international development particularly in Southeast Asia at Aoyama Gakuin University.

During the time at AGU, I studied in Thammasat University for 5 months, and volunteered in the Philippines to teach environmental issues to kindergarten students.
